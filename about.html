    Qualifications

    What's backing the coaching.

    USAT Certified

    USA Triathlon Level I certification — includes coaching liability coverage, SafeSport, and background check. [Update status once complete.]

    SafeSport trained

    Both coaches complete SafeSport training and a background check annually as part of USAT certification requirements.

    Real coaching reps

    3 years coaching a 20–30 athlete college club team to 5 races a season — not a first attempt at coaching other people.

    Our methodology

    We coach with your data, not a guess.

    The same approach that took us from below-average to professional racing — applied to every athlete on the roster.

    What we track

    • Power-to-heart-rate-to-cadence relationships, tracked over the season to catch fatigue and fitness shifts early
    • Training load week to week — not just whether a single workout hit a number
    • Aerobic-position data for triathletes, since aero demands differ from a normal ride
    • Pulled directly from your Garmin or Strava history

    What it means for your plan

    • Your plan is individually adjusted — not a fixed template handed to the whole roster
    • Check-ins are based on what your numbers actually show, not a generic schedule
    • Issues get caught in the data before they show up as a bad race
