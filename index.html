Why we exist

Three things we're actually trying to do.

Not a mission statement for a wall — these are the three problems we built the team to solve.

Spread the love

Build a real team

Camaraderie with amongst athletes enhances race week drastically — it changes mood, performance, and energy within each athlete. We build for that on purpose.

Travel and lodging keep athletes from racing across the country. We subsidize team kit and race-weekend housing so cost isn't what's holding you back.

The founders

We didn't start fast. We started exactly here.

Sury's first triathlon was a sprint at 9:30 mins/mile, biked under 16mph and swam 1:40/100 yards. He's since run low-6:00 pace and biked over 24mph and swam 1:15s/100 yards — and coached 20–30 athletes a year at Penn State's club triathlon team along the way.

Paarth's first triathlon was a sprint at 9:00 mins/mile, biked under 17mph and swam 1:35/100 yards. He's since run 5:45 pace and biked over 25mph and swam 1:15s/100 yards.
