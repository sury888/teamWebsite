Common questions

Before you ask, here's the honest answer.

Do I have to commit to a certain number of months?

No. Coaching is billed monthly and you can join or cancel any time. There's no minimum term.

Do I have to attend every team race?

No. Race weekends are opt-in per event — pay the flat room fee only for the races you actually attend.

What if the team grows too big for one house?

We book multiple houses near each other for that race weekend instead of one oversized property, so the room fee and the team experience stay the same regardless of how big we get.

Is the kit really only $50?

Yes — the kit costs $150 to produce, and we cover $100 of that for every athlete. You pay $50, once, and it's yours.

Are you actually certified to coach?

[Update once certification is complete] — we hold/are completing USA Triathlon Level I certification, which includes SafeSport training, a background check, and coaching liability insurance.
