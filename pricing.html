Common questions

Honest answers before you ask.

Do I have to commit to a certain number of months?

No. Coaching is billed monthly and you can join or cancel any time. No minimum term.

Do I have to attend every team race?

No. Race weekends are opt-in per event — pay the flat room fee only for races you actually go to.

What if the team grows too big for one house?

We book multiple houses near each other instead of one oversized property — the flat room fee and the experience stay the same.

Is the kit really only $50?

Yes. The kit costs $150 to produce and we cover $100 of that. You pay $50, once, and it's yours.
