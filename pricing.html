Common questions

Honest answers before you ask.

Do I have to commit to a certain number of months?

No. Coaching is billed monthly and you can join or cancel any time. No minimum term. To stay with a team during a race must be apart of the team for at least two months prior.

Do I have to attend every team race?

No. Race weekends are opt-in per event — pay the flat room fee only for races you actually go to.

What if the team grows too big for one house?

We will try to accomodate as many people as possible in the available houses. First come first serve for booking your spot in the house.

Is the kit really only $90?

Yes. The kit costs $200 to produce and we cover $110 of that. You pay $90, once, and it's yours.

Are you certified?

[Update once complete] — We are currently certified as a USA Triathlon Level I certification, which includes SafeSport, a background check, and coaching liability insurance.
